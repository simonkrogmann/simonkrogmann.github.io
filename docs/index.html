Simon Krogmann
game theory researcher, mathematician, computer scientist
intro publications bio

I investigate the impact of strategic behavior on

  • electricity markets: battery owners trade electricity which impacts grid stability (SAGT 2025)
  • facility location: facilities choose their locations, clients choose facilities to visit (thesis, AAMAS 2025, IJCAI 2024, AAAI 2023, IJCAI 2021)

and I create efficient algorithms for the above but also for

  • distance oracles: precomputed structures that speed up distance computations (Algorithmica 2026, TheoretiCS 2024, STOC 2023, ICALP 2023, WADS 2023)
