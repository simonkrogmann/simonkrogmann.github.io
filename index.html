Simon Krogmann
game theory researcher, mathematician, computer scientist
intro publications bio

I investigate how strategic behavior impacts systems like

  • electricity markets: battery owners trade electricity which impacts grid stability (SAGT 2025)
  • location choice: facilities choose their locations, clients choose facilities to attend (AAMAS 2025, IJCAI 2024, AAAI 2023, IJCAI 2021)

and I develop efficient algorithms for the above but also

  • distance oracles: precomputed structures that speed up distance computations (TheoretiCS 2024, STOC 2023, ICALP 2023, WADS 2023)
Mail: simon.krogmann@mail.uni-augsburg.de
